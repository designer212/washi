VSLA's

01. Micro-Irrigation Enterprise

We provide women smallholders with access to efficient, low-cost micro-irrigation assets, converting seasonal farming setups into highly commercial year-round businesses. This strategic structural upgrade enables female agricultural collectives to boost product consistency, maximize seasonal crop yields, and capture robust market pricing advantages independently.

WASH TRUST PROPERTY

02. Community Savings and Cooperative Lending Rings

To eliminate reliance on high-interest predatory micro-lending, our coordinators help organize autonomous community cooperative banks. Managed directly by rural women, these structural rings provide members with accessible, self-regulated capital pools, enabling them to fund technical gear and launch diversified micro-enterprises with ease.

05. Digital Financial Inclusion and Smart Tools

Technicians deliver specialized training workshops on mobile accounting setups, digital wallet networks, and basic commercial pricing tools. Connecting remote female business networks to regional digital marketplaces secures transparent record-keeping, minimizes transport costs, and opens access to expanded commercial supply lines.

WASH TRUST PROPERTY

06. Market Linkage Networks and Value Chain Alliances

The program coordinates direct wholesale supply alliances between remote rural women collectives and established institutional buyers, urban vendor markets, and commercial processing firms. Bypassing unverified middleman broker chains allows female producers to secure fair, long-term trade agreements and stable baseline profits.

WASH TRUST PROPERTY

07. Household Resilience and Diversified Livelihoods

By helping families diversify beyond a single subsistence harvest, our teams guide the establishment of small-scale eco-friendly businesses, organic livestock operations, and artisanal workshops. These varied alternative income networks protect household economies against climate shocks and localized crop failures.

WASH TRUST PROPERTY

08. Leadership Mentorship and Institutional Governance Audits

To secure a balanced share of community management control, we provide tailored training for women leaders within regional agricultural boards, water user boards, and civil committees. Cultivating robust skills in public speaking, strategic planning, and operational budget oversight ensures local development priorities are shared equitably.
