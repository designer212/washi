Financing Governance Policy

SECURE STEWARDSHIP PROTOCOL: To mitigate the severe risk of digital invoice interception, malicious domain cloning, and fiscal wire spoofing, WASH International Trust never publishes raw banking account credentials, routing numbers, or IBAN layouts on open web directories.

All global capital transfers, programmatic sub-grants, and institutional donations are processed through formal, encrypted Institutional Pledge Profiles. Verified corporate partners are provided with certified financing coordinates via verified secure institutional email endpoints.

Legal & Regulatory Verification

WASH International Trust operates as a fully incorporated development entity under the Trustees Incorporation Act of the Laws of Malawi. All programmatic project accounts are subjected to formal external audits annually.

Asset Tracking Transparency

100% of institutional grants received are linked directly to geospatial tracking markers. Donors are provided with precise GPS coordinates of engineered boreholes, VIP latrines, or active VSLA groups funded by their allocation.
