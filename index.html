Our Core Strategic Actions

WASH International Trust Malawi targets vulnerable community hubs, early childhood centers, and rural households to introduce localized, long-term self-supporting infrastructures.

The 8 pillars are interconnected in a strategic framework focusing on Climate Justice, Economic Empowerment and Resilience Building and Farmer Research and Knowledge Sharing.

Pillar 1
Pillar 1: Safe Drinking Water Supply

Safe Drinking Water Supply

Improving instant family access to clean drinking sources by establishing customized domestic and cluster pump assets built to withstand long-term usage.

Pillar 2: Organic Farming-Market Gardening

Organic Farming -Market Gardening

Pillar 3: Women Empowerment

Women Empowerment

Pillar 4: Improved Sanitation

Improved Sanitation
