Our Core Strategic Actions

WASH International Trust targets vulnerable community hubs, early childhood centers, and rural households to introduce localized, long-term self-supporting infrastructures.

1. Safe Water Supply & Hygiene

Improving instant family access to clean drinking sources by establishing customized domestic and cluster pump assets built to withstand long-term usage.

Learn More

2. Small-Scale Irrigation Systems

Equipping youth groups and community agricultural blocks with high-efficiency irrigation kits to secure robust food yields outside rain timelines.

Learn More

3. Women Economic Empowerment

Facilitating grassroots Village Savings and Loans Associations (VSLAs/Village Banks) to provide marginalized rural mothers and young caretakers with secure micro-capital access.

Learn Details

4. Ventilated Improved Sanitation

Building proper, highly adaptive family ventilated latrines to secure baseline rural sanitation and protect local shallow groundwater from chemical threats.

Learn More

5. Environmental Management

Enforcing rigorous catchment area protection, stabilizing local soil structures against seasonal runoff, and conducting ecosystem mapping to guard critical local aquifers.

Learn Details

6. Green Villages Campaign

Enforcing active community tree placement via regional seed banks, diverse agro-forestry actions, and sustainable honey bee farming assets.

Learn More

7. Geospatial Info & Mapping

Leveraging remote sensing platforms and precise digital data tracking setups to safely monitor physical asset lifespans and identify infrastructure gaps.

Learn More
