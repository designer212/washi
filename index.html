Our Core Program Pillars

WASH International Trust targets vulnerable community hubs, early childhood centers, and households to introduce low-cost, adaptive local solutions.

1. Safe Water Supply & Hygiene

Improving instant family access to completely safe drinking sources by establishing customized domestic and cluster pump assets built to withstand heavy usage.

Learn More

2. Small-Scale Irrigation Systems

Equipping youth groups and family collectives with highly specialized, affordable irrigation kits to secure food outputs outside standard rain timelines.

Learn More

3. Ventilated Improved Sanitation

Building proper, highly adaptive family ventilated latrines to secure baseline rural sanitation and protect local shallow groundwater from chemical threats.

Learn More

4. Green Villages Campaign

Enforcing active environmental stewardship via regional community seed banks, diverse agro-forestry actions, and local honey bee farming assets.

Learn More

5. Geospatial Info & Mapping

Leveraging remote sensing platforms and precise digital data tracking setups to safely monitor physical asset lifespans and identify infrastructure gaps.

Learn More
