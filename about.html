About Our Trust

The history, visionaries, leadership, and operational frameworks driving sustainable community transformation across Malawi.

Our Roots & History

WASH International Trust was founded out of a direct need to rewrite how community development models operate in Malawi. Established as an independent non-profit entity, the Trust was designed to address the deep, intersecting gaps within rural health, severe water accessibility problems, and economic vulnerability.

Rather than relying on short-term external relief measures, the organization focused on structural, long-term engineering methods: creating deep-aquifer water installations, biosecure sanitation facilities, and localized village financial institutions that remain fully functional long after construction wrap-ups.

Our Mission

To engineer community-managed clean water configurations, structural sanitation assets, and self-sustaining village micro-capital pools that break cycles of poverty across rural hubs.

Our Vision

A completely resilient Malawi where every village hub owns and runs its clean water, climate-smart agriculture networks, and secure financial security nets independently.

Strategic Goal

To implement complete, data-tracked physical asset frameworks and environmental monitoring installations across target rural communities by building clear structural transparency.
